          How is TruCite different from OpenEvidence?
          

            OpenEvidence and similar tools focus on retrieval and citations.
            TruCite is a verification and policy layer: it scores the output itself and returns an enforceable gate action that downstream systems can use to control AI-driven workflows.
          

        


        

          Is this just ‚ÄúAI correcting AI‚Äù? Isn‚Äôt that risky?
          

            TruCite does not replace your models or generate new content. It operates as an independent scoring and policy enforcement layer that supports human oversight and system-level safeguards.

            TruCite is model-agnostic. It focuses on output integrity and risk signals rather than internal vendor confidence scores. This allows TruCite to function as a stable verification and enforcement layer even as underlying models evolve.

            In this public demo, no. Enterprise deployments can support encrypted audit trails under strict retention controls.
