Founder Story

TruCite was founded by Mani Subramanian, MD, MMI ‚Äî physician executive, clinical informatics architect, and technology strategist ‚Äî to address a widening gap in the AI era: systems can now generate decisions, analysis, and guidance at scale, but there is no independent layer ensuring those outputs are reliable enough to trust in high-stakes environments.

After years leading clinical informatics, large-scale EHR transformation, and technology-driven care redesign, Mani saw firsthand how even small inaccuracies can cascade into serious consequences in domains like healthcare and law. As AI systems began entering these same environments, it became clear that generation alone was not enough ‚Äî AI outputs needed a verification layer that could evaluate reliability before they reached real-world workflows.

TruCite was created to be that layer. It is not a chatbot, search tool, or fact-checker. TruCite operates as independent verification infrastructure: evaluating structural integrity, evidence relevance, uncertainty signals, and domain risk to produce an auditable Truth Score and an enforceable decision outcome (ALLOW / REVIEW / BLOCK).

The goal is simple: transform AI from a source of hidden liability into a system that can be deployed with defensible, auditable reliability in the environments where correctness matters most.
