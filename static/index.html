FAQ's


      

        

          What does the TruCite symbol mean?
          

            The symbol is inspired in part by the Egyptian concept of Ma‚Äôat ‚Äî truth and balance ‚Äî
            where a feather is weighed against the heart. The golden feather represents truth, the
            blue orb represents signal being evaluated, and the curved arc represents the
            verification layer weighing evidence before a score is rendered.
          

        


        

          How is TruCite different from OpenEvidence?
          

            OpenEvidence (and similar tools) primarily focus on retrieval and citations ‚Äî surfacing
            sources to support an answer. TruCite is a verification decision layer: we evaluate the
            output itself for reliability (structure, internal consistency, uncertainty signals,
            drift risk) and return an audit-grade verdict.
          

        


        

          Is this just ‚ÄúAI correcting AI‚Äù? Isn‚Äôt that risky?
          

            TruCite does not replace your models or generate new content. It operates as an independent verification
            layer that evaluates AI outputs using structural integrity, uncertainty signals, and known risk patterns,
            and produces a human-reviewable explanation and audit trail.
          

        


        

          How is TruCite future-proof if models keep changing?
          

            TruCite is model-agnostic. It analyzes properties of the output itself ‚Äî coherence, structure, specificity,
            uncertainty cues, and drift vs historical outputs ‚Äî rather than relying on any one vendor‚Äôs internal confidence signal.
          

        


        

          Can TruCite detect model drift or silent behavior changes?
          

            Yes ‚Äî in enterprise mode. TruCite can track outputs over time for the same prompt or workflow and flag when
            a model upgrade or prompt change causes behavior to diverge.
          

        


        

          What exactly is a ‚Äútruth score‚Äù?
          

            The score is a composite reliability indicator ‚Äî not a magical oracle. It blends structural features (numerics,
            references, internal consistency), uncertainty signals, and known false-pattern heuristics to support a clear verdict.
          

        


        

          Why now? Haven‚Äôt we had fact-checking for years?
          

            Traditional fact-checking was built for human-written content in slow cycles. AI changes the tempo: millions of
            outputs across multiple models, updated frequently, embedded in agents and workflows. Verification must become a system.
          

        


        

          Why didn‚Äôt the big players build this themselves?
          

            Most platforms optimize for speed and scale. TruCite starts from the opposite direction:
            verification-first decisioning with auditability.
          

        


        

          Who is TruCite for?
          

            Teams deploying AI copilots or agents into production; institutions in legal, healthcare, finance, and
            compliance who need auditability; and AI vendors who want an independent verification layer.
          

        


        

          Does TruCite store my content?
          

            In this public demo, no. In enterprise deployments, TruCite can store encrypted audit trails under strict
            retention and access policies.
