          What does the TruCite symbol mean?
          

            The symbol is inspired in part by the Egyptian concept of Ma‚Äôat ‚Äî truth and balance ‚Äî
            where a feather is weighed against the heart. The golden feather represents truth, the

          How is TruCite different from OpenEvidence?
          

            OpenEvidence (and similar tools) primarily focus on retrieval and citations ‚Äî surfacing
            sources to support an answer. TruCite is a verification layer: we score the
            output itself (structure, internal consistency, uncertainty signals, drift risk) and return an audit-grade verdict.
          

        


        

          Is this just ‚ÄúAI correcting AI‚Äù? Isn‚Äôt that risky?
          

            TruCite does not replace your models or generate new content. It operates as an independent verification
            layer that scores AI outputs using structural integrity, uncertainty language, and known risk patterns.
          

        


        

          How is TruCite future-proof if models keep changing?
          

            TruCite is model-agnostic. It analyzes properties of the output itself ‚Äî coherence, structure, specificity,
            risk patterns, drift vs historical outputs ‚Äî rather than relying on one vendor‚Äôs internal confidence signal.
          

        


        

          Does TruCite store my content?
          

            In this public demo, no. In enterprise deployments, TruCite can store encrypted audit trails under strict
            retention and access policies.
