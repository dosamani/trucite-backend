FAQ's


      

        

          What does the TruCite symbol mean?
          

            The symbol is inspired in part by the Egyptian concept of Ma‚Äôat ‚Äî truth and balance ‚Äî
            where a feather is weighed against the heart. The golden feather represents truth, the
            blue orb represents signal being evaluated, and the curved arc represents the
            verification layer weighing evidence before a truth score is rendered.
          

        


        

          Is TruCite a fact-checker or a citation tool?
          

            No. Citation tools focus on retrieval and attaching sources. TruCite is a verification + enforcement layer:
            it evaluates output risk and returns an operational decision (ALLOW, REVIEW, BLOCK) under policy.
          

        


        

          What is the difference between a score and a Decision Gate?
          

            A score is informational. A Decision Gate is enforceable. TruCite provides both:
            a truth score/verdict for interpretability, and a policy-driven gate output that controls whether content can proceed automatically,
            must be reviewed, or is blocked.
          

        


        

          Is this just ‚ÄúAI correcting AI‚Äù? Isn‚Äôt that risky?
          

            TruCite does not replace your model or generate new content. It operates as an independent layer that inspects output for risk signals,
            requires evidence for high-liability claims, and enforces routing decisions. It is designed to reduce risk, not add another generative layer.
          

        


        

          How does evidence work in the MVP?
          

            In MVP, evidence is detected (URL/DOI/PMID) and checked for basic relevance via a heuristic (e.g., PMID appears in the claim or keyword overlap).
            Enterprise mode expands this into stronger evidence validation, drift analytics, and governance controls.
          

        


        

          Who is TruCite for?
          

            Teams deploying AI copilots or agents into production; organizations with compliance/risk oversight; and vendors who need an independent verification layer
            to make AI output auditable and enforceable.
          

        


        

          Does TruCite store my content?
          

            In this public demo, no. In enterprise deployments, TruCite can store encrypted audit trails under strict retention and access policies,
            aligned to governance requirements.
