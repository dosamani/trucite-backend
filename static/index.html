Why TruCite Exists

AI systems are rapidly being deployed into high-stakes environments — healthcare, law, finance, compliance — where incorrect outputs carry real-world consequences. Yet most AI systems provide no independent layer to assess reliability before decisions are made.

TruCite exists to bridge this gap. It serves as an independent verification layer that evaluates AI output for structural integrity, internal consistency, uncertainty patterns, and evidence signals — producing a defensible Truth Score and an enforceable Decision Gate action.

Where TruCite Is Used

Healthcare AI

Clinical AI systems can hallucinate studies, misstate dosing, or fabricate guideline references. TruCite evaluates output reliability before it reaches physicians, charts, or care pathways — helping prevent unsafe clinical decisions driven by AI error.

Legal AI & Research

Generative AI tools can produce fabricated case law, misquoted statutes, or unsupported legal reasoning. TruCite provides a verification layer and decision gate so unreliable outputs can be reviewed or blocked before entering briefs, contracts, or advisory workflows.

Financial & Compliance AI

AI-generated financial analysis may include incorrect figures, outdated regulations, or invented sources. TruCite helps ensure outputs meet reliability thresholds before being used in reporting, compliance, or risk-sensitive financial decisions.

How TruCite Works

1. Ingest

TruCite receives AI output, metadata (model, context), and optional sources.

2. Analyze

We inspect structure, numeric references, uncertainty language, citation signals, and known hallucination patterns.

3. Score & Gate

TruCite returns a Truth Score, decision action, and explanation for audit trails.
