Why TruCite Exists

AI can generate confident output at scale — but confidence is not correctness. In high-liability workflows, hallucinated facts, fabricated citations, and numeric errors create legal, clinical, and financial risk.

TruCite was built as the independent verification layer between AI capability and AI reliability — scoring output integrity and enforcing a Decision Gate before downstream use.

What TruCite Is (and Is Not)

TruCite is: a verification and enforcement layer that evaluates AI output and returns a Truth Score + Decision Gate action.

TruCite is not: a chatbot, search engine, or citation list. It does not generate answers — it evaluates reliability.

Where TruCite Fits

TruCite sits downstream of LLMs and agents, and upstream of high-risk use: contracts, clinical notes, financial reports, policy documents, and more. It complements retrieval systems — TruCite evaluates reliability, not just sources.
