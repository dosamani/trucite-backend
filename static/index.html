How TruCite Works


  

    
1. Ingest

    
TruCite receives AI output from models, agents, or enterprise systems — with or without citations.

  


  

    
2. Evaluate Reliability Signals

    
We analyze structural integrity, certainty language, numeric risk, hallucination patterns, and domain liability indicators.

  


  

    
3. Score & Gate

    
TruCite returns a Reliability Score and an enforceable Decision Gate action: ALLOW, REVIEW, or BLOCK.

  






  
Enhances AI Safety Without Replacing AI


  

    
Not a Fact-Checker

    
TruCite does not attempt to "know everything." It evaluates whether AI output is reliable enough to be trusted without human review.

  


  

    
Works Even Without Evidence

    
Most enterprise AI outputs driving decisions have no citations. TruCite evaluates risk and reliability regardless.

  


  

    
Evidence Improves — But Does Not Guarantee

    
Sources reduce risk, but AI can fabricate references. TruCite scores reliability beyond citation presence.

  


  

    
Built for High-Liability Environments

    
Legal, clinical, financial, and compliance outputs cannot rely on "probably correct." TruCite adds an accountability layer.

  






  
FAQ's


  

    Is TruCite just AI correcting AI?
    

      No. TruCite does not generate content. It evaluates reliability risk signals in AI output to determine whether human review is required.
    

  


  

    Does TruCite need citations to work?
    

      No. TruCite works even when no references exist. Evidence is one signal among many.
    

  


  

    What problem does TruCite solve?
    

      TruCite solves the AI accountability gap — ensuring AI output is evaluated for reliability before influencing real-world decisions.
