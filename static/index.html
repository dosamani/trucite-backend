Where TruCite Deploys First

TruCite deploys into high-liability workflows where AI output directly influences legal, clinical, financial, or operational action.

  • Legal AI Platforms — prevent fabricated citations or unsupported authority claims from reaching courts, clients, or regulators (bar sanctions, reputational exposure, liability risk).

FAQ

Is TruCite a model or a wrapper around LLMs?
No. TruCite is not a model and does not generate output.

It operates as an independent runtime layer that evaluates AI output and determines whether it is safe to execute in a real-world context.
Is this just fact-checking or citations?
No. TruCite does not attempt to improve or rewrite AI output.

It determines whether output is safe to act on — issuing a deterministic ALLOW / REVIEW / BLOCK decision based on execution risk.
If RAG or data pipelines are grounded, why is TruCite needed?
Grounding improves input quality — but does not guarantee execution safety.

TruCite evaluates output at runtime, detecting missing evidence, unsupported claims, and policy violations before downstream systems act.

It is the final execution decision layer, not part of the data pipeline.
Why can’t model providers solve this?
Model providers optimize for generation quality — not independent execution control.

TruCite operates outside the model boundary, ensuring decisions are not influenced by the same system generating the output.
Does TruCite execute actions?
No. TruCite emits a machine-readable decision and execution commit.

Customer-controlled systems enforce the decision boundary before execution.
What happens if TruCite is wrong?
TruCite is designed to be conservative in high-liability workflows.

REVIEW decisions allow human oversight, while BLOCK prevents unsafe execution.

All decisions are audit-bound for traceability and review.
Does this slow down systems?
No. TruCite operates at millisecond-scale latency and is designed for real-time workflows.
Is TruCite for consumers?
No. TruCite is designed for enterprise and regulated environments where AI output directly impacts real-world decisions.
